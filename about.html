    FAQs

    What is a Hackathon?
    A hackathon is a type of coding competition where teams have a short amount of time to work on creating a project.
    Why should I join a Hackathon?
    Hackathons are great experiences where you get to learn, create, collaborate, and compete in a friendly, low-stakes scenario. If you want to learn more about STEM or are interested in competing in other events, hackathons are great experiences for both beginners and experienced devs.
    Do I need to have expreience coding to join Mountain View Hacks?
    No, beginners are welcome. Mountain View Hacks was made to be beginner-friendly, and is open to any Mountain View student interested in competing and learning.
    What's the schedule for Mountain View Hacks?
    Mountain View Hacks will be held on Tuesday, March 14th, during part of the school day. We will have an opening ceremony, some workshops, as well as some time for students to work on their projects. After that, competitors will have until the end of Friday, March 17th, to submit their projects. About a week later, we will have a closing ceremony with prizes and results.
