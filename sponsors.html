    Our Mission Statement

    Our goal with Mountain View Hacks is to inspire the next generation of computer scientists by letting them get a hands-on experience with STEM and coding. In middle schools, not many students are exposed to real career activities that they could do; we hope to change this with Mountain View Hacks

    By donating and sponsoring Mountain View Hacks, you are showing the world that you care about the next generation of programmers, and that you want to help them get the resouces and experience that they need.

    For most students attending this hackathon, this is their first semi-competitive coding experience. We created Mountain View Hacks to be a beginner-friendly hackathon where they have the freedom and time to learn and explore different pathways in Computer Science, such as Web Development and Game Development.

    We hope that with your support, we will be able to give students a hands-on look into the CS field and let them experiment and learn with no limits.

    By sponsoring us, you have access to numerous perks, such as having your products used by attendees. Below is a breakdown of the different tiers of sponsorships we offer.

    Sponsorship FAQs

    Where will my money be going?
    Your donation will be going towards enhancing the experience for attendees. This could include purchasing prizes, software, etc. If we have any leftover money, it will either be saved for another hackathon, or will be donated to another non-profit or STEM club at Mountain View Middle School.
    Is my contribution tax-deductible?
    Yes! Mountain View Hacks is fiscally sponsored by Hack Club Bank, meaning we are a 501(c)(3) nonprofit. Your donation will be tax-deductible; it's a win-win for everyone!
    Why should I sponsor Mountain View Hacks?
    Aside from the package-based-benefits you will be getting from your donation value, you will also be showing the world that you care about young, aspiring developers! You'll be getting plenty of brand awareness and outreach to our attendees, as well as inspiring them with your products and businesses.
    What kind of sponsorships are you looking for?
    While we are primarily looking for monetary sponsorships, we would be grateful for any help you can provide. Swag donations, providing our attendees with tools, software, or hardware, and sharing your knowledge by supplying us with your beginner-friendly & pre-recorded workshops, as well as judges are all great ways to support our vision!

    Mountain View Hacks is fiscally sponsored by Hack Club Bank, a project by The Hack Foundation (d.b.a. Hack Club), a 501(c)(3) nonprofit (EIN: 81-2908499).
